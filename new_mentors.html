Each Season we have been blessed with amazing mentors. Our coaches guided us regularly for different aspects of our FLL journey, always motivating us through the challenges we faced. In addition we had mentors who brought in their specific expertise to help us in our Innovation Projects and learnings. We want to acknowledge their contribution to our team.

Matt Wilson

Mr. Mathew (Matt) Wilson

Mr. Wilson began his robotics career in 2016 with Robomatter, creating curricula for VEX and LEGO platforms and serving as a programming judge at VEX Worlds. He later mentored winning competitive teams, including the Gear Grinders, and has since expanded USC Robotics by guiding teams to state championships, running workshops, and supporting coaches locally and nationally. Now, as USC FLL Coordinator, he is committed to building a strong and thriving robotics community.

Past Coach

Mr. Mike Ressler

Mike Ressler, CTO of Diamond Kinetics and SidelineHD, has a long history of blending technology and youth sports, from founding StatEasy to coaching multiple teams in soccer, baseball, wrestling, and FLL robotics.
