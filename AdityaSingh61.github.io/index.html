About Us

Steel Synapse is a group of enthusiastic students who love exploring science, technology, engineering and math (STEM) through the First Lego League (FLL) program (Team #53463).

Our team is made up of seven members, each with unique skills and ideas eager to collaborate, learn, and work towards improving ourselves. Motivated and guided by our coaches to think creatively, we enjoy problem solving, designing robots and working with each other.

We believe teamwork and having fun are the important foundations to achieving our goals. Our mission is to grow as problem solvers, inventors and friends while making a positive impact through STEM.

Mission

We are a student-led robotics team based out of Upper St. Clair (USC). We are passionate about robotics since we believe that it is the future. We would like to make robotics accessible to every one so that it could change lives. We strive to make connections as strong as steel in our community. And to inspire other young innovators in hopes of building a bright future.
